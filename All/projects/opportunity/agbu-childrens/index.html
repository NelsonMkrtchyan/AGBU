Let the Space Fit the Dream
School is for learning about the world around us. Afterschool is for developing one’s character and self-purpose within. That’s why, after a quarter-century of success, AGBU is looking to keep its three Children’s Centers aligned with the future by funding a major overhaul of the interior spaces.

The Afterschool Reimagined Campaign is a $12 million facilities renewal initiative designed to ensure that no child is turned away from our children’s centers due to lack of capacity or resources. Within a timeframe of 3-4 years, our goal is to double student enrollment from "1,700" students currently to 4,000 across all three centers. These upgrades will also create the right conditions for expanding curricula and enhancing teaching tools.
Facilities Reimagined Each of the centers will share the same aesthetic sensibility and design philosophy:

  • Green eco-friendly construction materials
  • Open workspaces, natural lighting and versatile furnishings
  • Updated equipment, supplies, public safety and hygiene standards
  • Dedicated spaces for family and community events
  • A renovated Senior Dining Center facility housed on the premises
